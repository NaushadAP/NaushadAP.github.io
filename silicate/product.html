Sodium Silicate Uses

Textile

Textile

Sodium silicate is used in a variety of dyeing and water treatment processes in textiles: As a stabilizer or preserver for fiber reactive dyes such as Remazol. In bleaching process including hydrogen peroxide bleach stabilization And as a stabilizer and buffering agent to stabilize dyes that are reactive in nature in the Pad Dyeing process

Detergent and Soap

Detergent and Soap

Sodium silicate is used as a builder in powder detergents.It also aids as a water softener.

Auto Engineering

Auto Engineering

silicate is used as a cost effective binder in concrete to increase durability and waterproofing.

Steel and Foundry Industry

Steel and Foundry Industry

Sodium silicate is used as a binder in foundry cores for steel and casting industry.

Oil Drilling

Oil Drilling Industry

Silicate Based drilling fluids are used to stabilize welbores and are more environmentally friendly as compared to oil based drilling fluids.

Ceramics Industry

Ceramics Industry

Sodium silicate is used as a deflocculant in the ceramics industry.

Paper and Pulp Industry

Paper and Pulp Industry

Sodium silicate is used in the paper industry to improve the brightness of the pulp and Whiten the paper. It is also used in the bleaching process as a stabilizer for hydrogen peroxide during ink removal.

Adhesive Industry

Adhesive Industry

Sodium silicate is used as an adhesive in the packaging industry due to its cost competitiveness.
